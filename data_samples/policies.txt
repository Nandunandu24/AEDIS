=== CATEGORY 1: ORDER & SHIPPING ===

P3: Orders marked "Delivered" but reported as missing must be escalated to the Logistics Team.

P9: Address change requests for "Pending" orders can be auto-resolved.

P14: Inquiries about "Pickup in store" availability must be auto-resolved based on current inventory API.
P15: Requests to combine two separate orders into one shipment must be escalated.
